# World Cup 2026 Sweepstake

A live league table tracking win probabilities for our World Cup 2026 sweepstake.

Each player was randomly assigned four national teams. The table ranks players by the combined probability of any of their teams winning the tournament, sourced from the [Opta Supercomputer](https://theanalyst.com/competition/fifa-world-cup/predictions). Probabilities are updated automatically every 2 hours.
